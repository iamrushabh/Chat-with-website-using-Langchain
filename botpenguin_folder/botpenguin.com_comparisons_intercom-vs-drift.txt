What is Drift?
Drift is a conversational marketing platform that enables businesses to engage and qualify leads through chatbots and live chat.
Key features include AI-powered chatbots, lead generation, appointment scheduling, integrations, and analytics.
Alternatives to Drift include BotPenguin, Intercom, HubSpot Chat, and Freshchat, which offer similar conversational marketing capabilities for engaging and qualifying leads across various channels.
Pros: Utilizes AI-powered chatbots, lead generation tools, appointment scheduling, integrations, and analytics.
Cons: pricing and customisation options may pose challenges, and it is not a dedicated chatbot suite.

What is Intercom?
Intercom is a communication platform that streamlines business interactions with customers through in-app messaging, email, and live chat services.
Intercom alternatives that shine in customer support and engagement include platforms like BotPenguin, Help Scout, HelpCrunch, Olark, HubSpot Services Hub, Zendesk and Freshdesk.
Intercom's pricing can be prohibitive for small businesses and startups, making it difficult to access its powerful features on a limited budget.
Pros: Easy-to-use interface, personalized messaging, fast customer support, live chat features, robust automation, and app integrations.
Cons: Relatively expensive, limited customization options, additional costs for extra features, UI experience can be overwhelming for some users.

Frequently Asked Questions
How does the pricing for Drift vs Intercom compare?
Drift costs significantly more than Intercom. In fact, Drift's pricing is approximately three times higher per month than Intercom's annual cost.
Do Drift and Intercom offer multi-platform integration support?
Both Drift and Intercom have limited platform integration options. They do not offer support for WhatsApp, Facebook, and Telegram, unlike BotPenguin which supports these and more.
Can you create more than one chatbot with Drift or Intercom?
Both Drift and Intercom limit users to one bot per account. BotPenguin, on the other hand, allows you to create as many bots as you need.
Do Drift and Intercom offer voice support for their chatbots?
Fortunately, both Intercom and Drift provide voice support for their chatbots.
Is it possible to schedule drip campaigns for customer onboarding on Drift and Intercom?
Both Drift and Intercom lack this feature specifically for Facebook and Telegram, while BotPenguin offers a feature for scheduling drip campaigns for customer onboarding on these and other platforms.
