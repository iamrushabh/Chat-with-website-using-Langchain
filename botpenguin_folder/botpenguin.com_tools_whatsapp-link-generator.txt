Frequently Asked Questions
How do I get my WhatsApp link?
To get your WhatsApp link, input your phone number with the country code and create a custom message. Then, click "Generate My Link."
How do I share and send my WhatsApp number Link?
Share your generated WhatsApp link via social networks, email, or text messaging so that clients can connect with you on WhatsApp.
How to add a WhatsApp link to my website?
Embed the generated WhatsApp link in your website as a button or by inserting it in the HTML code, allowing users to message you directly.
How to create a WhatsApp link?
Input your phone number, including the country code, create a custom message, and click "Generate My Link" to create your WhatsApp link.
How much does it cost to create WhatsApp links?
Creating WhatsApp links is generally free, as many online generators offer the service at no cost.
What is the WhatsApp custom message?
A WhatsApp link starts with "https://wa.me/" followed by your phone number, including the country code, and a custom message.
What occurs when someone clicks on the WhatsApp link?
Clicking on the WhatsApp link opens a chat with your predefined custom message, enabling users to connect with you instantly.
Is there a cost associated with creating a WhatsApp link?
No, creating a WhatsApp link is generally free using online generators.
Does a WhatsApp link work in my country?
Yes, a WhatsApp link works worldwide as long as users have an active internet connection and can access WhatsApp.
Can I embed a WhatsApp link in an image?
Yes, you can embed a WhatsApp link in an image by tying the link to the image`s HTML code or using an image map.
Why do I need a WhatsApp QR code?
A WhatsApp QR code streamlines connecting with users as they can simply scan the code to initiate a chat with your custom message.
Is WhatsApp link available for WhatsApp Business App users?
Yes, the WhatsApp link feature is available for both regular and WhatsApp Business App users, facilitating direct communication with customers.
