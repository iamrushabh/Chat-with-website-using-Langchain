Frequently Asked Questions
What is BotPenguin's Chatbot Reseller Program?
BotPenguin's Chatbot Reseller Program is a partnership program where individuals or businesses can become affiliates, implementation partners, or white-label chatbot resellers to offer AI chatbot solutions to their clients.
What are the benefits of becoming BotPenguin's Chatbot Reseller Partner?
By joining BotPenguin's Chatbot Reseller Program, partners can increase their revenue with cross-selling opportunities, recurring revenue streams, and uncapped commissions. Based on their chosen program, Partners also get access to exclusive dashboards, training materials, support, and expert tech and customer success teams.
Who is eligible for BotPenguin's Chatbot Reseller Program?
Marketing agencies, consultants, freelancers, technical solution providers, YouTube vloggers, web studios, and social media influencers are eligible to join BotPenguin's Chatbot Reseller Program.
What services does BotPenguin offer to its partners?
BotPenguin offers a drag-and-drop chatbot builder platform, a unified inbox, and a dedicated partnership manager for its partners. Implementation partners and white label partners also get access to sales and tech experts, and robust service support. Affiliates get their own dashboard to keep track of their earnings.
How can BotPenguin's chatbot reseller partners earn money?
BotPenguin's partners can earn money through commissions, referrals, cross-selling to existing clients, and recurring revenue streams.
How can I join BotPenguin's Chatbot Reseller Program?
Interested individuals or businesses can join BotPenguin's Chatbot Reseller Program by filling out a form on our website and selecting the desired partnership level. A dedicated partnership manager will then contact them to start the onboarding process.
