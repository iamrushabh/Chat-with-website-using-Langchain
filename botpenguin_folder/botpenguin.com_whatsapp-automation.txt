Frequently Asked Questions
What is WhatsApp Automation?
WhatsApp Automation allows businesses to engage with customers through automated chats on WhatsApp, providing customer support, marketing, and sales services efficiently.
Can I integrate WhatsApp Automation with my existing tools?
Yes, you can. Platforms like BotPenguin help you integrate seamlessly with different CRM systems, ticketing systems, and customer segmentation tools, allowing you to streamline your workflows.
How can WhatsApp Automation help boost my sales?
WhatsApp Automation helps collect customer details, manage sales, share product catalogs, engage leads with live chat, and follow up post-sales, facilitating a complete sales journey on WhatsApp.
Can I provide 24/7 customer support with WhatsApp Automation?
You can provide round-the-clock support by training BotPenguin to handle FAQs and integrating them with live chat for complex query resolution handled by live agents.
Do I need coding skills to use WhatsApp Automation on BotPenguin?
No, BotPenguin is designed to be user-friendly and requires no coding skills. You can build and deploy WhatsApp chatbots using our intuitive drag-and-drop builder.
Is there a free plan available for WhatsApp Automation?
BotPenguin offers a Free Forever plan that allows you to start using WhatsApp automation features without any cost or credit card requirement.
