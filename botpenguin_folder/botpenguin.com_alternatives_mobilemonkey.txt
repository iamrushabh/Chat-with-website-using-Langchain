Frequently Asked Questions
Is MobileMonkey a chatbot?
Mobile Monkey is a CX platform that collects and analyzes customer data, manages relationships, and automates interactions, improving satisfaction with targeted segmentation. It also allows users to create and manage chatbots.
How much does MobileMonkey cost?
The pricing for MobileMonkey (Customers.ai) is $199 per month. Pro plans goes upto $499 per month. Pricing coupled with limited features & channels becomes a determining factors for users to seek MobileMonkey alternatives.
Is MobileMonkey free?
MobileMonkey does offer a free plan, but it is limited to only 50 contacts.
What is the best MobileMonkey alternative?
The alternative to MobileMonkey mentioned in the content is BotPenguin. BotPenguin is a better multi-channel AI chatbot, offering access to WhatsApp and Telegram. It has a visual flow builder, 60+ native integrations, including Wix, Shopify, & WooCommerce, 40+ business-ready chatbot templates, and more.
Does Customers.ai offer an API?
Yes, Customers.ai, does offer API, allowing you to connect your chatbot to external apps. Using the API keys, you can send messages in text or chat, update contacts, retrieve contacts data, and more.
