What functionalities does the hotel booking chatbot template include?
The hotel booking chatbot template supports room bookings, reservation management, special package inquiries, customer support, and general hotel information.
Can the chatbot handle multiple languages?
Yes, the chatbot can be configured to support multiple languages to cater to an international guest base.
How does the chatbot integrate with our existing booking system?
The chatbot can be integrated with your current booking system through APIs or by connecting with booking management tools.
Is the chatbot customizable to reflect our hotel’s branding?
Absolutely. The chatbot’s appearance, tone, and responses can be customized to match your hotel’s brand identity.

How can a chatbot maker enhance our hotel booking chatbot?
