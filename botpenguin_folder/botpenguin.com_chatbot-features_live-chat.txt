Frequently Asked Questions
What is Live Chat Online and how does it help my business?
Live Chat Online lets you interact with website visitors in real-time. It helps you quickly answer questions, guide customers, and close sales faster, creating a better user experience.
Can I use Live Chat on my website for free?
Yes, BotPenguin offers a free Live Chat solution for websites. You can engage with your visitors without any upfront costs, providing excellent customer service right from your site.
Is BotPenguin’s Live Chat app secure?
Absolutely! Our Live Chat app is secure, ensuring all your conversations and customer data are protected with encryption, giving you peace of mind.
Can I use Live Chat with a chatbot?
Yes, BotPenguin offers a Live Chat with chatbot feature, allowing the bot to handle simple queries while agents focus on more complex conversations, boosting efficiency.
How does BotPenguin's Free Live Chat software work?
Our Free Live Chat software integrates seamlessly with your website or app, enabling you to communicate with customers in real-time without needing to install complicated tools.
Does the BotPenguin Live Chat app work on mobile?
Yes, our Live Chat app works across all devices, including mobile. You can stay connected with your customers no matter where you are, offering timely support on the go.
