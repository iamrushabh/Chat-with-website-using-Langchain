What is model customization, and why is it important?
Model customization refers to tailoring the ChatGPT model to specific business needs or domain expertise. It involves fine-tuning and personalizing the model's responses to ensure they are accurate and relevant to the user's queries. Customization is important because it helps businesses increase the efficiency and accuracy of the model.
What are ChatGPT plugins?
ChatGPT plugins are tools that expand ChatGPT's capabilities, allowing it to access real-time information, perform computations, or integrate with third-party services for a more customized and powerful chatbot experience.
