So, why do you need a Zendesk alternative?
Pricing: Zendesk offers full chatbot functionality starting from their professional plan, which costs $115 an agent per month.
AI Addons: Zendesk’s native chatbot doesn’t have AI or intent suggestions. So, you need to buy an add-on at $50 per month.
Learning Curve: Being a comprehensive platform, it takes a fair bit of time to master for first time users.
Not a Chabot: Yes, Zendesk is not a chatbot but a CRM tool. The chatbot is one of its features, which is why it costs an arm and a leg.
Low Customization: Zendesk Answer Bot has limited customization options, making it difficult to match a business's brand guidelines or specific needs.
Different Bots, Different Roles: Zendesk has a bot for customer service and one for sales. Zendesk alternatives like BotPenguin offer customer support, eCommerce, lead generation, etc., in one chatbot.

Frequently Asked Questions
How does Zendesk Answer Bot work?
Zendesk Answer Bot uses a combination of predefined responses and machine learning to automate responses to customer inquiries. It analyzes the text of the inquiry, identifies relevant articles from the knowledge base, and suggests them as possible solutions.
Can you recommend a cost-effective alternative to Zendesk?
Yes, BotPenguin is a cost-effective alternative to Zendesk. It offers conversational AI chatbot features, training, omnichannel live chat, and 60+ integrations at a significantly lower price of $50 per annum.
Is there a free alternative to Zendesk available?
BotPenguin is a free Zendesk alternative. It offers a free lifetime plan with 2000 monthly messages. It provides access to various features social media and website chatbots, chat flow, FAQs upload, quick replies, and more, making it a cost-effective option compared to Zendesk.
Is there a Zendesk alternative that has similar functionalities?
Yes, BotPenguin is a Zendesk alternative with almost similar chatbot features. BotPenguin offers support for ticketing systems, live chat, team management, chatbot builder, conversational AI, and other chatbot features.
Which company or platform is a direct competitor to Zendesk?
BotPenguin is a direct Zendesk alternative. It offers similar customer support functionalities, AI chatbot features, 40+ templates, and 60 native integrations at a more affordable price point.
In terms of pricing, which Zendesk alternatives offer a more budget-friendly option?
BotPenguin stands out as a more budget-friendly alternative to Zendesk, with an annual cost of $50 compared to Zendesk's $588. Other alternatives like Freshdesk and Zoho Desk also offer more affordable pricing options.
What are the drawbacks or disadvantages associated with using Zendesk?
Some drawbacks of using Zendesk include high pricing, limited customization options for the Answer Bot, the need to purchase additional add ons for AI functionality, and a learning curve for first-time users due to its comprehensive platform.
