Costly Customer Support: Nothing is for free. Tawk.to doesn’t charge you upfront, but it’s support is pretty expensive, negating the free factor.
Analytics: Tawk.to has limited reporting and analytics, which makes it challenging to track customer interactions and enhance live chat performance.
Chatbot Customization: Users find it troublesome to customize the chatbot widget. Maintaining uniform branding is tricky, especially for new users.
