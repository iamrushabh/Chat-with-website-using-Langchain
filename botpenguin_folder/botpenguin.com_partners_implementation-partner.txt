Frequently Asked Questions
What is BotPenguin's Implementation Partner Program?
BotPenguin's Implementation Partner Program allows individuals and companies to partner with BotPenguin and resell chatbots to their clients. As a partner you can offer your customers Chatbots with 80+ native integrations, including ChatGPT. You can also offer omnichannel access with generative AI features and earn commissions on every sale.
Who all can join BotPenguin's Chatbot Reseller Program?
Freelancers, web studios, technical solutions providers, Youtubers, social media influencers, and marketing agencies can join BotPenguin's Implementation Partner Program and resell chatbots to their customers.
What generative AI features does BotPenguin’s chatbot platform have?
BotPenguin’s chatbot utilizes ChatGPT’s model to render AI features like web and custom data set scrapping, FAQs upload, import customer interactions, auto-language translation, contextual responses, and more. .
What do I need to do as an implementation partner with BotPenguin?
As a chatbot reseller, you have to purchase a paid annual plan. Then, We will set up your agency dashboard and you can start selling right away!
What are the benefits of becoming an implementation partner with BotPenguin?
Becoming a chatbot implementation partner with BotPenguin comes with several benefits, including offering a top-notch product, ChatGPT’s conversational powers, the ability to create your own subscription plans, charge implementation fees, and having access to expert tech assistance. As an implementation partner, you can create a recurring revenue stream and sell add-ons, white-label chat windows, and integrations to your clients to earn more.
What methods can be utilized to generate passive income through chatbot sales?
Explore avenues like reselling, offering add-ons, and introducing chatbots to packaged deals within BotPenguin's chatbot reseller program.
