What is a Chatbot?
A chatbot is a computer-assisted interface that allows a person to acquire information from a machine.
The chatbot's interface is generally textual (chat), and in many situations, the user is given information in the form of a simple multiple-choice option.
Chatbots
are utilized in several channels for multilingual customer service, lead creation, and sales automation (e.g. Facebook Messenger, websites, WhatsApp, SMS).
There are plenty of excellent platforms from which to pick.
Some of them have additional advantages and are simpler to utilize. Different chatbot builders aid in achieving various objectives, therefore let's start looking for your ideal answer.
