Frequently Asked Questions
What is the difference between WhatsApp and WhatsApp API?
WhatsApp is a personal messaging app for individual communication, while WhatsApp API is designed for businesses, enabling automated messaging, large-scale communication, and integration with CRM systems to enhance customer interactions.
How can you create a WhatsApp chatbot for your business?
To create a WhatsApp chatbot, sign up with BotPenguin, integrate with WhatsApp API, and design a logical chat flow. Incorporate AI for human-like responses, and use Meta-approved message templates for initiating conversations.
How much does WhatsApp marketing cost?
WhatsApp marketing costs depend on region and conversation volume. Meta charges per conversation, while BotPenguin offers services at no additional markup and includes free green tick verification for your business account.
Is WhatsApp marketing suitable for small businesses, and how can it be implemented?
WhatsApp marketing is ideal for small businesses. Implement it by using WhatsApp Business or API, sending personalized messages, and automating customer support, leading to high engagement and conversion rates.
How is AI being utilized in WhatsApp marketing and automation?

Can you show Shopify products catalog in WhatsApp?
To display Shopify products on WhatsApp using BotPenguin, first integrate Shopify with Meta. Then, go to Meta Commerce and sync your product catalog from Shopify. BotPenguin will then allow you to share product catalogs directly in WhatsApp inboxes.
