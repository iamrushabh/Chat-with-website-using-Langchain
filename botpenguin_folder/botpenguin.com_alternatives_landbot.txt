Who are the competitors of Landbot?
Some competitors of Landbot in the chatbot market include BotPenguin, Chatfuel, Botpress, etc. These platforms offer similar functionalities and are considered as Landbot alternatives.
Does Landbot have a free substitute?
No, there are no free Landbot alternatives but it offers a free trial version with limited features. However, you can get BotPenguin, which is 9x more affordable and provides better chatbot features.
What is the best Landbot substitute for whatsap chatbots?
BotPenguin is the ideal Landbot alternative to create Whatsapp chatbots. With BotPenguin, you can create an AI-enabled WhatsApp bot in minutes for Bookings, lead generation, customer support etc. This chatbot comes with conditional flows, chatGPT integration, FAQs import, quick replies and more.
Is Landbot pricing based on the number of chatbots?
Landbot's pricing is primarily based on the number of interactions and features needed, rather than the number of chatbots. Different pricing plans offer varying levels of functionality and usage allowances. Landbot alternative like BotPenguin follow a similar pricing model.
How many native integrations Landbot chatbot offers?
Landbot enables users to integrate with 40+ integrations, including Slack, WhatsApp, Hubspot, Strip, WordPress, Salesforce, Zendesk, etc. It integrates with a decent number of applications and platforms. However, landbot alternatives like BotPenguin offer more native 60+ integration.
