Frequently Asked Questions
Is Tidio chatbot worth it?
Tidio's pricing model can be unsuitable for most businesses, making it expensive. It also lacks features like WhatsApp API integration, Telegram chatbot, Facebook comments auto-reply, live chat ticketing, and detailed analytics that other chatbot platforms provide. Therefore, switching to a Tidio alternative like BotPenguin can be better.
Which chatbot provider is the perfect alternative to Tidio?
BotPenguin is a better choice than Tidio in terms of pricing, automation, integrations, conversational AI, and omnichannel support. Hence, it is the best Tidio alternative out there.
Is Tidio a good chatbot?
Tidio is a decent chatbot platform, but compared to alternatives like BotPenguin, it lacks certain valuable features such as 60 native integrations, detailed analytics, Facebook automation, and WhatsApp API integration.
Does Tidio support WhatsApp?
Tidio does not offer WhatsApp API integration, essential for setting up sophisticated WhatsApp chatbots tailored to your business-specific use case. On the other hand, Tidio alternatives like BotPenguin do offer scalable WhatsApp chatbots capable of broadcasts, drip chat flows, campaigns, sales, appointment booking, ticketing, etc.
Is there any free alternative to Tidio?
BotPenguin offers a free lifetime plan that includes 2000 monthly messages with 30 days of data storage, making it a good Tidio alternative.
