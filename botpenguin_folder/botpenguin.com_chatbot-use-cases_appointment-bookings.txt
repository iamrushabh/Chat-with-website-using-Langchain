Frequently Asked Questions
How do I make an appointment with chatbot?
Follow these steps to create a scheduling chatbot with generative AI for your business: - Login to BotPenguin. - Click on “Create a New Bot”. - Select the purpose as “Appointment Booking”. - Name your chatbot and configure its avatar. - And you are done. - Set up the condition flow and integrate it with your ChatGPT account to enable the AI features.
Can I integrate the slotting bot with external applications?
Yes, you can integrate with 80+ third-party applications, including ChatGPT, Zoho, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, and more. Our appointment booking chatbot also integrates with Pabbly and Zoho, enabling you to connect your generative AI bot with over 1000 apps.
Can I integrate the booking chatbot with my Google Calendar?
Yes, effortlessly sync BotPenguin's appointment chatbot with Google Calendar for efficient scheduling and automated updates.
Can I use this Appointment Booking template for free?
Absolutely! BotPenguin offers a FREE Forever Plan for the appointment booking chatbot, no credit card required. You get the basic features, 2000 messages, and unlimited contacts. You can also use the ChatGPT features for upto 100 messages. Afterward, integrate your own ChatGPT account to use the AI at full scale.
How does ChatGPT help with appointment booking?
ChatGPT enhances appointment booking by providing a conversational interface, answering queries, and making the scheduling process seamless and user-friendly.
Can I sell event tickets from the Appointment Booking Chatbot?
Yes, you can integrate your scheduling chatbot with your preference booking platform and sell event tickets & collect payments in chat. After successful booking, you can send tickets as images or PDFs within the chat interface.
