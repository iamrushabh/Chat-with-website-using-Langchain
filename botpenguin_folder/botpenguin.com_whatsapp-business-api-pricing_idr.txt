Conversation Categories used by Meta
WhatsApp Business platform conversations fall into
four categories that are priced differently
Marketing
Marketing conversations involve sharing promotions, offers, updates, or invitations that encourage customers to respond or take action.
Utility
Service conversations help businesses assist customers with inquiries, resolve issues, and address general questions or concerns.
Service
Utility conversations handle specific requests or transactions, providing updates on ongoing activities, confirmations, or post-purchase notifications.
Authentication
Authentication conversations verify users with one-time passcodes, often used at various stages like account verification, recovery, or security checks.
Frequently Asked Questions
Is there a free plan?
Yes, we have a free plan with 100 conversations.
What is your markup on Meta prices?
We do not charge anything on top of meta conversation charges, you pay WhatsApp conversations fee directly to Meta.
Is there any message limit?
No, all charges are conversations based. BotPenguin’s platform pricing is based on the plan of conversations you choose.
I want a WhatsApp bot along with my website bot, what should I do?
You will have to take two separate subscriptions, as WhatsApp has a different pricing structure.
Can you provide me with green-tick verification?
Yes, we do, once you get started with us, we'll get you through with everything.
Can I send bulk messages?
Yes, they can be scheduled and targeted as per your strategy.
